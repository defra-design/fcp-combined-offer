SSSI consent

If you apply for actions or items on land that’s a site of special scientific interest (SSSI), you must get SSSI consent from Natural England.

You must get this consent before you do your selected actions or items on SSSI land. Some actions or items can be done without SSSI consent. You can check which actions this applies to in the Find funding for land or farms tool. You can check which items this applies to in the Capital grant finder.

Read the guidance on SSSI consent.

If you are not applying for actions or items on SSSI land, or the actions or items can be done without SSSI consent, answer ‘yes’ to continue your application.  

If you require SSSI consent, you must get it before you do any of the actions or items on this land. Will you do this?
