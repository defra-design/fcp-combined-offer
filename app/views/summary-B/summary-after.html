Summary of actions

  • Print

SFI actions are more straightforward and mostly won’t need specialist advice.

Higher tier actions are more complex, and need specialist support and advice.

You might want to submit a separate application for higher tier actions. These take long to process and will delay the SFI part of your agreement.
