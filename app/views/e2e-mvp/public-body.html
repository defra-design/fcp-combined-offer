Land owned by a public body

Land owned by some public bodies is eligible for this scheme as long as the work does not form part of their obligations as a public body.

This includes land owned by:
• local authorities
• national park authorities
• public corporations

Parish councils and former college farms are not public bodies and are eligible for this scheme.

If you’re a tenant of land owned by a public body, you will need to check with your landlord if the land is eligible for an agreement.
