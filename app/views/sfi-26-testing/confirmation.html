Request a SFI Historic Environment Farm Environment Record (HEFER)

You’ve applied for SFI actions on land with historic or archaeological features. This means you must request an SFI HEFER from Historic England before you do your SFI actions on this land. Read the SFI26 scheme information on what a SFI HEFER is and how to get one

Get site of special scientific interest (SSSI) consent

You've applied for actions or items on land that's a site of special scientific interest. Once you've received and accepted your agreement offer, you must get SSSI consent from Natural England before you start your selected actions or items on this land. Read the guidance on SSSI consent and how to get it.
