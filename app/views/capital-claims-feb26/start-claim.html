Claim for a CSHT Tier capital item

Each capital item claim you make must be at least £500, unless it is your final claim. Your final claim can be less than £500. The RPA may reject your claim if it does not meet these requirements. For this form you'll need:

  • to check the capital grant finder to see what evidence you need to send to the RPA depending on the capital item you are claiming for
  • details of any public funding you've received for the item you want to claim for
  • proof of your VAT status

For your VAT status, you'll need evidence from HMRC or your accountant which either confirms:

  • you are not registered for VAT
  • you are registered for VAT but you cannot claim VAT from HMRC for capital items
