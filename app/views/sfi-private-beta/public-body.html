Public bodies

If you’re not a public body, or you’re not a tenant on land owned by a public body, answer 'yes' to continue your application.

If you're a public body, you can apply for an SFI agreement. But you will not be paid for activities that:

  • you're required to carry out as part of your statutory duties
  • No longer used
  • public corporations

Read the guidance about the eligibility of public bodies.
